## Active Lifestyle

A healthy body fuels a creative mind. Sports teach discipline, resilience, and consistent effort. Staying active is essential to who I am.

---

## Activities

- **Cycling** — Exploring the city on two wheels. Freedom on the road, meditation in motion.
- **Running** — Building endurance, clearing the mind, pushing personal limits.
- **Basketball** — The thrill of team sport, competition, strategy, and joy with friends.
- **Mountain Hiking** — Conquering trails, fresh air, discovering Colombian landscapes.
- **Chess** — Strategic thinking, patience, the beauty of calculated moves. A gym for the mind.

---

## Gaming

A lifelong companion. Favorites include The Legend of Zelda and Hollow Knight. Gaming is interactive art, storytelling, and design appreciation. A great way to unwind after long days of coding.

---

## Reading & Sci-Fi

Drawn to science fiction books and film. Isaac Asimov is a favorite author. Time travel, dystopian futures, space exploration — these stories fuel imagination and offer fresh perspectives on technology's role in humanity.

---

## Finding Balance

The best ideas come when stepping away from the screen. Cycling, hiking, and chess spark creativity. In tech, it's easy to get caught up in work. I've learned that stepping away, pursuing hobbies, and maintaining physical and mental health are essential for sustainable productivity and happiness.
