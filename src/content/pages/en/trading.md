## My Trading Journey

What started as curiosity about how financial markets work became a deep interest in forex — the largest and most liquid market in the world. Two years dedicated to studying market dynamics, understanding how macroeconomic events affect currency prices, and developing a personal trading approach. The discipline challenges me daily.

---

## My Approach

Systematic, data-driven, and built on a disciplined mindset. I combine fundamental analysis (news, economic data, geopolitical events) with technical analysis (charts, patterns, indicators). Risk management is central — every trade has a clear plan with entry, stop loss, and take profit levels. A detailed trading journal tracks performance over time.

---

## What I Study

- **Forex Trading** — Currency pairs, market sessions, central bank policies, macro drivers
- **Algorithmic Trading** — Automating strategies, backtesting, building trading bots with programming
- **Market Analysis** — News impact, economic indicators, sentiment analysis, global events
- **Risk Management** — Position sizing, stop-loss strategies, risk-reward ratios, capital protection

---

## Trading Philosophy

Trading is discipline, not gambling. Success comes from education, patience, and emotional control. The market rewards consistency and punishes impulsiveness. My tech background gives me an edge in combining engineering with trading — the goal is to understand markets deeply and build intelligent decision-making systems.
