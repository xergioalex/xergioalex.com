## My Entrepreneurial Journey

Started with a Computer Science degree and a passion for building. The path went from freelancing to co-founding DailyBot, a Y Combinator (S21) company. Along the way, lessons in product development, team building, and creating value for users.

## Ventures & Projects

### DailyBot (YC S21) — 2017–Present
CTO & Co-founder. Building a team collaboration platform used by thousands of teams worldwide. Selected for Y Combinator S21.

### Pereira Tech Talks — 2016–Present
Co-founder. Building a tech community in Colombia that brings developers, designers, and entrepreneurs together to share knowledge.

### Open Source & Tools — Ongoing
113+ GitHub repositories. Contributing to the developer ecosystem with open source projects, tools, and libraries.

## My Philosophy

Technology is a force for change. The best builders are problem solvers who understand their users. Success comes from relentless value delivery with humility. Community is at the heart of everything meaningful.

## Key Lessons

1. Talk to your users — their feedback is worth more than assumptions
2. Move fast, but build things that last
3. Surround yourself with people better than you
4. The best time to start is now — perfectionism is the enemy
5. Community and collaboration accelerate everything
6. Stay curious — the best ideas come from unexpected places
