## 1. Definitions & Fundamentals

### What is AEO?

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) is the practice of optimizing content so that AI platforms provide direct answers to queries and cite your content as a source, instead of just listing links.

- **SEMrush:** "A set of marketing practices used to increase your brand's visibility in AI-generated answers — like Google's AI Mode and ChatGPT responses." — [SEMrush: What Is Answer Engine Optimization?](https://www.semrush.com/blog/answer-engine-optimization/)
- **HubSpot:** "When someone asks ChatGPT for marketing advice, queries Google for a quick definition, or speaks to Alexa about local services, AEO determines whether your brand is cited in the response." — [HubSpot: What is AEO?](https://blog.hubspot.com/marketing/answer-engine-optimization)
- **Ahrefs:** "Traditional search is about competing for clicks, while AI search is about being cited within the answer itself." Also referred to as GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) or LLMO (Large Language Model Optimization). — [Ahrefs: Answer Engine Optimization](https://ahrefs.com/blog/answer-engine-optimization/)

### AEO vs Traditional SEO

| Dimension | Traditional SEO | AEO |
|-----------|----------------|-----|
| **Goal** | Rank high in SERPs | Be cited in AI-generated answers |
| **Content format** | Long-form pages | Tight, structured answer blocks extractable by AI |
| **Optimization target** | Keywords and backlinks | Entities, questions, conversational queries |
| **Query type** | Short keyword phrases | Longer, natural-sounding questions |
| **Success metrics** | Rankings, CTR, traffic | Brand mentions, citation frequency, sentiment |
| **Traffic model** | Click-dependent | "Visibility without traffic" — cited thousands of times without generating a single analytics session |

**Key insight:** AEO will not replace SEO. Strong SEO fuels AEO — AI systems favor content from trusted domains with strong organic rankings. — [HubSpot: AEO vs SEO](https://blog.hubspot.com/marketing/aeo-vs-seo), [SEMrush: AEO vs SEO](https://www.semrush.com/blog/aeo-vs-seo/)

### AEO as Evolution of SEO in the Age of AI Agents

The shift from "10 blue links" to AI-generated answers has been gradual:

- **2012:** Google Knowledge Graph — answered ~1/3 of searches by 2016. — [Search Engine Land](https://searchengineland.com/guide/knowledge-graph)
- **2014:** Featured Snippets — "the ten blue links were definitively a thing of the past." — [Search Engine Land](https://searchengineland.com/guide/featured-snippets)
- **May 2023:** Google SGE announced at Google I/O. — [SE Ranking](https://seranking.com/blog/ai-overviews/)
- **May 2024:** Rebranded as "AI Overviews" and rolled out in the US. — [Google Blog](https://blog.google/products-and-platforms/products/search/generative-ai-google-search-may-2024/)
- **May 2025:** Expanded to 200+ countries, 40+ languages. — [Evergreen Media](https://www.evergreen.media/en/guide/google-ai-overviews/)
- **2026:** AI Overviews use Gemini 3 globally, serving over a billion people. — [Google Blog](https://blog.google/products-and-platforms/products/search/ai-mode-ai-overviews-updates/)

Search Engine Land describes AI Overviews as "the next evolutionary leap from simple extraction (like the featured snippet) — where search engines pull exact text from web pages — to intelligent synthesis, where AI combines and reinterprets information from multiple sources." — [Search Engine Land](https://searchengineland.com/google-ai-overviews-evolution-revolution-443454)

**Emerging term — AAO (Assistive Agent Optimization):** The next evolution focusing on AI agents that take actions on behalf of users. — [Search Engine Land](https://searchengineland.com/aao-assistive-agent-optimization-469919)

### AEO vs GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)

The term GEO was introduced in a November 2023 paper by Princeton, Georgia Tech, Allen AI, and IIT Delhi researchers. Demonstrated GEO strategies can boost visibility by up to 40%. Published at KDD 2024. — [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.09735)

Views diverge on whether they're the same:
- **"Same thing":** "AEO, GEO, and GSO all mean the same thing." Some prefer AEO because "GEO conflicts with geography." — [TryProfound](https://www.tryprofound.com/blog/aeo-vs-geo)
- **"Different targets":** AEO targets Google AI features (format problem); GEO targets AI chatbots (substance problem). — [Neil Patel](https://neilpatel.com/blog/geo-vs-aeo/)
- **Ahrefs** uses them interchangeably. — [Ahrefs: GEO Guide](https://ahrefs.com/blog/geo-generative-engine-optimization/)

### Google AI Overviews

- Uses existing Google Search index. No separate crawler needed. — [Google Docs](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/ai-features)
- Uses "query fan-out" technique — a page at position 40 for a sub-query can end up cited. — [Google Blog](https://developers.google.com/search/blog/2025/05/succeeding-in-ai-search)
- 86% of citations come from pages ranking in top 100 of Google. — [Ahrefs](https://ahrefs.com/blog/search-rankings-ai-citations/)
- Only 12% of cited URLs rank in top 10 for the original prompt. — [Ahrefs](https://ahrefs.com/blog/ai-search-overlap/)
- Content with 134-167 word self-contained units preferred for citation.
- Pages combining text + images + video + structured data see 156% higher selection rates. — [Wellows](https://wellows.com/blog/google-ai-overviews-ranking-factors/)

### Bing Copilot

- Uses Bing search index (Bingbot). — [Bing Webmaster Blog](https://blogs.bing.com/webmaster/February-2026/Introducing-AI-Performance-in-Bing-Webmaster-Tools-Public-Preview)
- AI Performance report launched Feb 2026 — first tool giving publishers visibility into AI citations.
- Shows: total citations, average cited pages, grounding queries, page-level citations. — [Search Engine Land](https://searchengineland.com/bing-webmaster-tools-ai-performance-report-468751)

### Perplexity AI

- Two crawlers: PerplexityBot (indexing, NOT for training) and Perplexity-User (real-time). — [Perplexity Docs](https://docs.perplexity.ai/guides/bots)
- Hybrid retrieval: lexical + semantic + vector embeddings.
- Favors: topical precision, factual density, recency, source diversity, clean extractability. — [RankShift](https://www.rankshift.ai/blog/how-to-get-cited-as-a-source-in-perplexity-ai/)
- Top cited domains: Reddit (6.6%), YouTube (2%), Gartner (1%). — [Exposure Ninja](https://exposureninja.com/blog/ai-search-statistics/)

### ChatGPT / SearchGPT

- Three crawlers: GPTBot (training), OAI-SearchBot (search index), ChatGPT-User (real-time). — [OpenAI Docs](https://platform.openai.com/docs/bots)
- Each controllable independently — allow search while blocking training.
- Supplements own crawling with Bing's index. — [Withdaydream](https://www.withdaydream.com/library/how-openai-crawls-and-indexes-your-website)
- Top cited: Wikipedia (7.8%), Reddit (1.8%), Forbes (1.1%). — [Exposure Ninja](https://exposureninja.com/blog/ai-search-statistics/)

### Claude (Anthropic)

- Three bots: ClaudeBot (training), Claude-SearchBot (search), Claude-User (real-time). — [Anthropic Help](https://support.claude.com/en/articles/8896518-does-anthropic-crawl-data-from-the-web-and-how-can-site-owners-block-the-crawler)
- Web search powered by Brave Search. — [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2025/03/21/anthropic-appears-to-be-using-brave-to-power-web-searches-for-its-claude-chatbot/)
- All three bots respect robots.txt including Crawl-delay.

### Apple Intelligence

- Developing "World Knowledge Answers" — expected in iOS 26.4. — [Search Engine Land](https://searchengineland.com/apple-world-knowledge-answers-ai-search-461569)
- Two bots: Applebot (search), Applebot-Extended (AI training evaluation). — [Apple Support](https://support.apple.com/en-us/119829)
- Using Google's Gemini models for parts of AI search (~$1B/year deal).

## 4. Standards & Specifications

### llms.txt

- **Creator:** Jeremy Howard (Answer.AI), September 3, 2024.
- **Official spec:** [llmstxt.org](https://llmstxt.org/) | [GitHub](https://github.com/AnswerDotAI/llms-txt)
- **What it is:** Markdown file at `/llms.txt` providing LLM-friendly site summary at inference time.
- **Adoption:** ~10% across 300K domains analyzed; 844K+ sites total. — [Rankability](https://www.rankability.com/data/llms-txt-adoption/)
- **Notable adopters:** Anthropic, Cloudflare, Vercel, Supabase, ElevenLabs.
- **Reality check:** No major LLM provider has confirmed their crawlers consistently read it. Google's John Mueller (June 2025): "No AI system currently uses llms.txt." — [Semrush](https://www.semrush.com/blog/llms-txt/), [LinkBuildingHQ](https://www.linkbuildinghq.com/blog/should-websites-implement-llms-txt-in-2026/)

### Schema.org Structured Data

- **Official:** [schema.org](https://schema.org/) | [Google Structured Data docs](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/intro-structured-data)
- **JSON-LD is Google's recommended format.** — [Google Docs](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/sd-policies)
- Pages with schema markup earn **2.8x higher AI citation rates**. — AirOps research
- **March 2025:** Google, Microsoft, and OpenAI confirmed they use schema for generative AI. — [Stackmatix](https://www.stackmatix.com/blog/structured-data-ai-search)

**Most relevant types for AEO:** Article, BlogPosting, Person, Organization, FAQPage, WebSite, BreadcrumbList, Speakable.

**Note:** FAQ rich results restricted to authoritative gov/health sites since Aug 2023. HowTo deprecated. — [Google](https://developers.google.com/search/blog/2023/08/howto-faq-changes)

### E-E-A-T

- **Official:** [Google Quality Rater Guidelines](https://guidelines.raterhub.com/searchqualityevaluatorguidelines.pdf) | [Creating Helpful Content](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/fundamentals/creating-helpful-content)
- **Experience** (added Dec 2022), **Expertise**, **Authoritativeness**, **Trustworthiness** (most important).
- E-E-A-T verification became 27% stricter in 2025. — [Evergreen Media](https://www.evergreen.media/en/guide/answer-engine-optimization/)
- Content lacking E-E-A-T gets filtered before AI consideration.

### robots.txt AI Directives

- Each AI provider has separate training vs. search crawlers. Block training while allowing search. — [Paul Calvano](https://paulcalvano.com/2025-08-21-ai-bots-and-robots-txt/)
- ~21% of top 1,000 websites have rules for GPTBot.
- AI training crawling = ~80% of all AI bot activity (Cloudflare 2025 data). — [Cloudflare](https://blog.cloudflare.com/from-googlebot-to-gptbot-whos-crawling-your-site-in-2025/)
- OpenAI verifiable IP ranges: `openai.com/gptbot.json`, `openai.com/searchbot.json`.

### Emerging Standards

- **IETF AIPREF Working Group** (chartered Feb 2025): Standardizing preferences for AI content collection/processing. Draft `draft-ietf-aipref-vocab-04`. Three categories: Foundation Model Production, AI Output, Search. — [IETF Blog](https://www.ietf.org/blog/aipref-wg/)
- **ai.txt proposal:** Domain-specific language for AI interactions, beyond allow/block. Very early (19 GitHub stars). — [GitHub](https://github.com/menro/ai.txt)

## 5. Best Practices

### Content Structure

- Place core answer in first 40-60 words of a section. — [HubSpot](https://blog.hubspot.com/marketing/answer-engine-optimization-best-practices)
- 120-180 word sections between hierarchical headers → 40% citation improvement. — [LLMrefs](https://llmrefs.com/answer-engine-optimization)
- Lists, tables, and structured formats parsed more reliably than prose. — [CXL](https://cxl.com/blog/answer-engine-optimization-aeo-the-comprehensive-guide/)
- Semantic HTML matters — AI crawlers usually don't render JS. — [JetOctopus](https://jetoctopus.com/the-2026-technical-seo-playbook-optimization-for-ai-crawlers-agents/)

### Structured Data

- JSON-LD required. Pages with schema have 2.5x higher AI citation chance. — [AirOps](https://www.airops.com/blog/schema-markup-aeo)
- Sites with structured data + FAQ blocks: 44% increase in AI citations (BrightEdge). — [Search Engine Land](https://searchengineland.com/schema-ai-overviews-structured-data-visibility-462353)

### Author Authority (E-E-A-T)

- Author bios, Person schema with `sameAs` to LinkedIn/GitHub, verified credentials. — [T-Ranks](https://t-ranks.com/seo/eeat-signals/)
- Cite sources explicitly. Include first-hand experience, original research.
- Adding statistics with methodology notes: 22-28% visibility improvement. — [Hook Agency](https://hookagency.com/blog/brand-authority-ai-search/)

### Content Freshness

- 76.4% of AI-cited pages updated within previous 30 days. — [Ten Speed](https://www.tenspeed.io/blog/content-freshness-aeo-era)
- AI platforms prefer content 25.7% fresher than traditional search results. — [Hill Web Creations](https://www.hillwebcreations.com/content-freshness/)
- Visible "last updated" timestamps → 30% more Perplexity citations. — [Averi AI](https://www.averi.ai/blog/google-ai-overviews-optimization-how-to-get-featured-in-2026)

### Multilingual AEO

- Properly localized sites: up to 327% more AI Overview visibility vs single-language. — [Koanthic](https://koanthic.com/en/multilingual-seo-ai/)
- Machine-translated content without localization underperforms.
- Three layers needed: SEO, AEO, GEO/LLM. — [Weglot](https://www.weglot.com/academy/multilingual-seo-and-geo-for-beginners)
